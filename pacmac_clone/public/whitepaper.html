VibeCode Whitepaper

Democratizing software creation through AI.

1. Introduction

VibeCode is a revolutionary platform designed to empower individuals and small teams to transform their ideas into functional web applications instantly, without requiring any coding knowledge. Our mission is to break down the barriers to software development, making it accessible to everyone, from aspiring entrepreneurs to creative individuals.

2. Vision

Our vision is a world where anyone can build. We believe that the future of software creation lies in intuitive, AI-powered tools that translate natural language into robust applications. VibeCode aims to be the bridge between imagination and execution, fostering a new generation of digital creators.

3. Technology Stack

While the current iteration is a static site, our long-term vision incorporates a robust technology stack:

  • Frontend: React with Vite and Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: Node.js with Express.js (TypeScript)
  • Database: PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM
  • Authentication: Passport.js (GitHub OAuth)
  • AI Integration: OpenAI and other leading AI models

4. Features

  • No-code, AI-powered app & website generation
  • Free subdomains for instant deployment
  • Serverless, scalable architecture
  • Community-first collaboration features (future)

5. Roadmap

Our roadmap includes expanding AI capabilities, integrating more third-party services, enhancing collaboration tools, and developing a comprehensive marketplace for AI-generated components and templates.

6. Conclusion

VibeCode is more than just a tool; it's a movement to democratize technology. Join us in building the future of software creation.
